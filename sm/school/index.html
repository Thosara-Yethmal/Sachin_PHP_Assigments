About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

ABC International School is a very well-regarded, high achieving and popular school located across 10 major cities in Sri Lanka. It is a fully-fledged K12 co-educational school network with English as the primary medium of instruction in teaching and learning. At ABC, we provide a foundation for life in a happy, high achieving school, where all individuals have high expectations of themselves, respect each other and strive to excel. The school offers a wide range of experiences based on a progressive curriculum, extra-curricular activities and involvement with the wider community.
